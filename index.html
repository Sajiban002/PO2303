Paris

Date: 2024-06-15

During the trip to Paris, I explored the iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre Museum. The breathtaking view from the top of the Eiffel Tower was unforgettable, and the boat ride along the Seine river offered a scenic view of the city. I also indulged in delicious French cuisine at local cafes and bistros, and experienced the charming atmosphere of Montmartre.

New York

Date: 2024-05-20

My visit to New York was filled with exciting experiences. I explored the bustling streets of Times Square, visited iconic landmarks like the Statue of Liberty and Central Park, and enjoyed panoramic views of the city from the Top of the Rock observation deck. Attending a Broadway show was a highlight of my trip, and I was captivated by the energy and talent of the performers. I also sampled diverse cuisines at local restaurants and food markets, immersing myself in the vibrant food scene of the city.
